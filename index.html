HERE ARE SOME VARIATIONS OF OUR PRODUCT

Industrial Settling Tank


A robust, industrial-grade tank designed to efficiently separate solids from liquids. Ideal for various industrial applications, including wastewater treatment and chemical processing.

Vertical Clarifier


A sleek, vertical clarifier that effectively removes suspended solids from liquids. Its compact design and efficient operation make it a popular choice for both small-scale and large-scale operations.

Thickener Tank


A specialized tank designed to concentrate suspended solids in a liquid slurry. It is commonly used in wastewater treatment plants to reduce sludge volume and improve overall treatment efficiency.
